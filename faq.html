FAQ

How does it work?

It’s really simple (like all good inspiration!), just pick your subscription (monthly, or prepay), then each month we will send you a carefully curated box that includes items to help you live your most inspired life. Sign up easily and cancel anytime!

What will be in my box?

Each month we choose a different inspirational theme and products that go along with that.

Can I send a box as a gift?

Absolutely! Inspiration is such a wonderful gift, we’re sure whomever you gift this to will be delighted. When you check out, you will fill out your billing information, then at the bottom of that section, simply choose “Ship to a different address” and you can enter your gift recipient’s address. We do not include a packing slip with the box, so they won’t ever see your payment information.

My Inspiration Box arrived damaged, what do I do?

We’re so sorry! Contact us by email and our customer care team will figure out a solution right away!
